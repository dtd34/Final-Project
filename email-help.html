How to Enable Email Links (mailto:)

If clicking an email link doesn’t open Gmail, follow these steps:

  1. Click the three vertical dots at the top right of your Chrome window.
  2. Go to Settings → then on the left, click Privacy and security.
  3. Click Site Settings, then scroll and expand Additional permissions.
  4. Select Protocol handlers(Fourth Option Down).
  5. You'll see something like this:
  6. Click the X next to Gmail to remove it.
  7. Now open Gmail in a new tab.
  8. Look for a diamond icon in the address bar (far right by the magnifying glass).
  9. Click it and choose Allow Gmail to open all email links.
  10. You're done! Email links will now open directly in Gmail.
